Further Reading

Further reading on the Spanish Civil War:

  • The Battle for Spain: The Spanish Civil War 1936-1939 – Antony Beevor
    A good overview of the Spanish Civil War.
  • Homage to Catalonia – George Orwell
    An excellent first-hand account of the war from possibly the most famous international volunteer.

Further reading on Free and Open-Source software:

  • The Free Software Foundation (FSF)
    https://www.fsf.org/
    A nonprofit working to promote freedom respecting software worldwide.
  • Electronic Frontier Foundation (EFF)
    https://www.eff.org/
    Another nonprofit working to defend digital rights.
